ABOUT LAUNCHX

LaunchX is a pan-India level fund-raising event that not only aims to bring EMINENT STARTUPS across the nation but also beings in WORLD-RENOWNED MENTORS, thus throwing a spotlight on some of the most promising startups in the country. Through overall leveraging of the entire entrepreneurial ecosystem LaunchX gives the startups an OPPURTUNITY to Launch their Start-ups and at the same time develop necessary skills. The Start-up Pitch competition would be providing fundrasing oppurtunities and provide a tremendous pool of market oppurtunities and resources available. LaunchX exclusively provides early-stage Start-ups the platform to shine and get under te limelight of the Eco-system.
